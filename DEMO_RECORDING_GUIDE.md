# 🎥 TrustLink Demo Recording Guide

As requested by Satyam Shukla, this guide provides the absolute best, 100% free ways to record a high-quality, professional `.mp4` demo video of the TrustLink platform for LinkedIn.

## 🛠 Top Recommended Free Tools

### 1. OBS Studio (Best for Professional LinkedIn Demos)
**Why:** It is 100% free, open-source, leaves no watermarks, and exports directly to pristine 1080p `.mp4` files.
**How to use:**
1. Download [OBS Studio](https://obsproject.com/).
2. Open OBS and add a new "Display Capture" or "Window Capture" source targeting your Google Chrome/Edge browser.
3. In `Settings > Output`, set the Recording Format to **mp4**.
4. Click **Start Recording**, walk through the TrustLink app, create a link, submit feedback, and showcase the responsive design.
5. Click **Stop Recording**. The `.mp4` is now in your Videos folder, ready to be uploaded straight to LinkedIn!

### 2. Loom (Best for Quick Narrated Demos)
**Why:** Extremely easy Chrome extension that records your screen and camera simultaneously.
**How to use:**
1. Install the [Loom Chrome Extension](https://www.loom.com/).
2. Click the Loom icon and hit "Record".
3. Walk through the dashboard features.
4. When finished, Loom provides a link, but you can also **download the video as an .mp4** for free from their web interface to post securely to LinkedIn.

### 3. Windows Native Recorder (No Installation Required)
**Why:** Built directly into Windows.
**How to use:**
1. Open TrustLink in your browser.
2. Press `Windows Key + G` to open the Windows Game Bar.
3. Click the "Record" circle button. 
4. The output is saved directly as a crisp `.mp4` file in your `C:\Users\YourUser\Videos\Captures` folder.
